13

Globe — Malaysia outreach

Orthographic globe initially centred on Malaysia (Shah Alam HQ). Malaysia is highlighted in brand orange; every other landmass sits in a muted brand-blue tone. Each "rocket-launch" line draws outward from Shah Alam on a quadratic-bezier arc that lifts above the chord (longer flights arc higher), holds, then fades and re-launches. The animation is staggered so the field constantly fans out. Drag to rotate — animations keep running through the drag (path elements are reused; only the geometry updates per frame). No auto-rotate. Drop-in candidate to replace the spinning globe in the homepage's "Build on expertise" section.

Live on the homepage. Draggable. Rocket-line animation continues during drag.

15

Product Overview — popup hotspots (prototype)

Redesigned interaction for the homepage Product Overview. Clicking a numbered hotspot reveals an image card that sits right next to the number, overlaying the drawing — above the number for hotspots 1, 2 and 4 (top half), below for 3 and 5 (bottom half). The card's transform-origin is anchored to the number's column so the scale animation reads as the image "emerging from the number". Click the same hotspot to dismiss, click another to switch, click outside or press Esc to close. No side info card — just the image.
