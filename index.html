COMPANY PROFILE

Build on expertise.
Driven by innovation.

Furutec Electrical Sdn Bhd is a manufacturer of busduct systems, specialising in indoor, outdoor, data centre, lighting and power monitoring solutions. With over 31 years in the manufacturing and R&D of busduct systems in Malaysia, we deliver certified, reliable systems trusted by engineers, developers and project owners worldwide.

Our products are type-tested and certified by reputable certification bodies, ensuring full compliance with international standards, while our comprehensive range of busduct systems is thoughtfully tailored to meet diverse applications and market segments.

Power For You.

From single‑line review to commissioning, our four‑stage handover process is designed to leave a consultant, facility manager and client equally confident on day of switch‑on.

01

Site survey and load analysis — we review your single‑line, cooling and growth plan before a single copper bar is quoted.

02

Engineering review with your consultants: short‑circuit, voltage drop, IP rating, fire barrier and trace‑heat are locked in.

03

Manufacture, type‑test witness and factory acceptance — every joint torque‑marked and photo‑logged for QA records.

04

On‑site hoisting, jointing and commissioning by factory crew, with full IR, HiPot and thermographic reports at handover.
